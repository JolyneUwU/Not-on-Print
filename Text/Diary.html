+++ 1 month of weekly Uploads +++

01.02.2026

 

I did it:)

After last year when I was taking a very long break because of the intense launch of the website, I am now very happy to report that the first month of 2026 I managed to keep a routine for the first month. The idea to have a set day on which I just upload whatever I have so far finished really seems to work for me. I wrote not daily but still regularly. Instead of just throwing ideas away when they get too big or I fail at executing them I now finish some small projects every week. That keeps me motivated. I consider maybe taking off a week from time to time if that helps me focus on bigger projects, but probably not in February.

 

Anyways I want to share some thoughts on what I wrote so far. The Red Stream chapter one developed differently from what I had originally planned. But the idea of keeping it shorter and ending it on a cliffhanger opened better options for chapter two then what I initially planned so I am confident in that decision. Places is fun and works really well against writer's blockade. I hope there is something there for the readers. Everything else was mostly fun:)

 

Also I was informed that Google autotranslated text for some readers. I deactivated it. When I want text translated in my opinion it would be necessary for a human to translate otherwise, there is just no control over tone and other nuances.

 

That's it. Looking forward to the future of notonprint, currently I am sitting on a folder to apply for a university in creative writing again. I might as well post updates on that.

 

Sincerely Kassandra <3
